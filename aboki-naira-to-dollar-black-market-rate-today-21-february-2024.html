    How much is a dollar to Naira today in the black market?

    Dollar to naira exchange rate today black market (Aboki dollar rate):

    On Wednesday, the 21st of February 2024, the Lagos and Abuja Parallel Market, also known as the Black Market, reported an exchange rate of ₦1,800 to a dollar for buying and ₦1,825 for selling. These figures are based on information obtained from Bureau De Change (BDC) sources.
