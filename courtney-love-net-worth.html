    When did Hole form and what was their debut album?

    Hole was formed in 1989 in Los Angeles by Courtney Love and guitarist Eric Erlandson. Their debut album, Pretty on the Inside, was released in 1991.

    What are some of Hole’s most notable albums?

    Besides their debut, Hole’s critically acclaimed albums include Live Through This (1994) and Celebrity Skin (1998), the latter receiving three Grammy nominations.

    Has she had a solo music career?

    Yes, she released her first solo album, America’s Sweetheart, in 2004 and continued to produce music, including her latest album after a 12-year hiatus, described as a magnum opus.
