    Can you trust eDreams?

    eDreams has a consumer rating of 1.08 stars from 504 reviews indicating that most customers are generally dissatisfied with their purchases. Consumers complaining about eDreams most frequently mention customer service, credit card and return flight problems.

    How do I get my money back from eDreams?

    Once you have informed us of your need to cancel the flight, we will process the refund and send you an e-mail letting you know how much will be credited to the card you used to purchase the tickets. This credit will be automatic, however, every refund is different and depends on the type of ticket and the airline.
