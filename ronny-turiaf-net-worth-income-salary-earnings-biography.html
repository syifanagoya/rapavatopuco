    Ronny Turiaf (born January 13, 1983) is a French professional basketball player who currently plays for the National Basketball Association’s Minnesota Timberwolves (NBA). Turiaf has played basketball for the French national team. Turiaf grew raised in Martinique, then went to high school in Paris and college in the United States at Gonzaga University.  Turiaf was a member of the Gonzaga Bulldogs basketball team and was the top scorer in the West Coast Conference his senior year. Turiaf entered the 2005 NBA Draft after graduating from Gonzaga and was selected by the Los Angeles Lakers, where he would play until 2008. Later in his career, he was a member of the Golden State Warriors, New York Knicks, Washington Wizards, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Clippers.
